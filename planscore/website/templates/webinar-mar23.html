March 23 Webinar

In advance of the upcoming (March 28) SCOTUS arguments in the gerrymandering case Benesik v. Lamone, we’re hosting a quick online demonstration of PlanScore, the new, easy-to-use redistricting assessment tool devised by gerrymandering and coding experts.

If you’d like to join, and send us your questions in advance, you can register below.

PlanScore allows citizens and journalists to upload proposed state districting maps and instantaneously have them scored for partisan symmetry. It also offers historical data for state house and U.S. congressional district plans from 1972 to the present.  

On Friday, March 23, attorney Ruth Greenwood and PlanScore Executive Director Michal Migurski will demonstrate how to use PlanScore in the context of current developments around gerrymandering. Ruth is Senior Legal Counsel at the Campaign Legal Center and co-counsel in Gill v. Whitford. Michal is a geospatial code and data expert who recently served as chief technology officer of Code for America
